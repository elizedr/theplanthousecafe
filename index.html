About Us

The Plant House Cafe is a space to foster hope, creativity, and community. A space where belonging is felt from the first greeting to the last sip. Since the beginning, The Plant House Cafe creates a space for people to call home. Freshly backed pastries, hand-brewed coffees, toasty warm teas, and authentic interactions carry the essence of home throughout the space, with passion and joy at the core.
